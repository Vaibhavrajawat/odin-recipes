Butter Paneer Recipe

Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular street food from Mumbai, India. It consists of a spicy mashed vegetable curry (Bhaji) served with soft buttered rolls (Pav). This dish is flavorful, filling, and a favorite for many. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients

For Bhaji:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, cauliflower), chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

For Pav:

  • 8 pav buns
  • Butter, as needed

Steps to Prepare

Preparing Bhaji:

  • Boil the mixed vegetables until soft. Drain and mash them well. Set aside.
  • Heat oil and butter in a large pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for a few minutes.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and bell pepper. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil separates.
  • Mix in pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, and turmeric powder. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add the mashed vegetables and mix well. Add water to achieve desired consistency and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Stir in lemon juice and adjust salt to taste.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro.

Preparing Pav:

  • Slice the pav buns horizontally and butter the insides generously.
  • Heat a flat pan and toast the buns until they are golden brown and crispy on the inside.

Serving

Serve the hot Bhaji with buttered Pav. Garnish the Bhaji with a dollop of butter, chopped onions, and a wedge of lemon. Enjoy this delicious Pav Bhaji with your family and friends!
